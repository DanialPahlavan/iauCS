Practice Exercise

Create a registration form with the following fields:

  1. Username (required, 3-20 characters)
  2. Email (required, valid email format)
  3. Password (required, minimum 6 characters)
  4. Confirm Password
  5. Gender (radio buttons)
  6. Favorite subjects (checkboxes: Math, Science, Literature)
  7. Country (dropdown)
  8. Comments (textarea)
  9. Terms agreement (required checkbox)
  10. Submit button

Use proper labels, fieldsets for grouping, and validation attributes.
